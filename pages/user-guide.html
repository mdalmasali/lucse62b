Getting Started

No Login Required
1
Go to the Portal
Open your browser and visit lucse62b.xyz. You will land on the Home page. Some sections are public, but most features require you to be logged in.
2
First Time? Set Your Password
If you have never logged in before, click Login in the top navbar, then click "Set Password" on the login page. Enter your 16-digit Student ID and follow the steps to create a new password.
3
Log In
Click Login in the navbar. Enter your Student ID (16 digits) and your password, then click Login. Once logged in, the navbar will show all available pages.
4
Forgot Password?
On the login page, click "Forgot Password". You will need to verify your identity using your Student ID and date of birth, then you can reset your password.
Your login session is saved in the browser. You do not need to log in again every time you visit, unless you clear your browser data or log out manually.

Home Page

Public
  • Navigation Grid — Quick-access tiles to all major sections of the portal: Students Directory, Classwork, Class Info, Resources, Result Dashboard, Cover Page, Gallery, and What's New.
  • Exam Countdown Widget — Shows a live countdown to the next university exam. Appears 7 days before the exam date so you always know how much time is left.
  • Memories Section — A scrollable collection of class photos and memorable moments from the batch.
  • Contact Info — Department and batch contact details at the bottom of the home page.

Students Directory

Login Required
1
Search for a Student
Use the search bar at the top to find a classmate by name or Student ID. Results update instantly as you type.
2
View a Student Profile
Click on any student card to open their full profile. You can see their name, ID, photo, department, and other details.
This page is only accessible after logging in. Unauthenticated users are redirected to the login page automatically.

Classwork

Login Required
1
Browse by Course
The Classwork page shows all enrolled courses as cards, grouped by semester. Click a course card to open its materials.
2
Browse by Category
Inside each course you will find materials organized by category — Assignments, Tutorials, Lab Reports, Viva, Presentations, and more. Click a category to see the list of files.
3
Open or Download a File
Click any file in the list to open or download it. Files are served from Google Drive and open in a new tab.
All classwork data is synced live from Google Sheets by the class management team. If a new file has been uploaded, it will appear here without needing a page refresh.

Class Info

Login Required

The Info page has multiple sections, accessible from the left sidebar menu:

  • Class Routine — The current weekly class schedule with time, room, and teacher details. Updated whenever the routine changes.
  • Course List — All courses offered in the current semester with credit hours and teacher assignments.
  • Bus Schedule — University shuttle bus timings and routes relevant to CSE 62B students.
  • Teacher Contacts — A directory of course teachers with their office information and contact details.
  • Exam Schedule — Upcoming exam dates and times for each course. A countdown timer is also shown on the Home page when an exam is within 7 days.
All information on this page is pulled live from Google Sheets and reflects the most up-to-date data maintained by the class representatives.

Resources

Login Required
1
Search for a Resource
Use the global search bar at the top to find study materials by keyword — book name, subject, or topic. Results update as you type.
2
Browse by Category
Resources are organized into categories such as Books, Slides, Notes, Past Papers, and more. Click a category tab to filter the list.
3
Download or Open
Click a resource card to open the file. If a download button is available, click it to save the file to your device.
4
Load More
If there are more resources than displayed, a Load More button appears at the bottom. Click it to load the next batch of items.

Result Dashboard

Login Required
1
Enter Your Details
On the Result Dashboard page, enter your Student ID, select your Semester, and enter your Date of Birth (used to verify identity with the LU system).
2
View Your Results
Click Check Result. Your semester results will be fetched directly from the Leading University result system and displayed with subject-wise grades and GPA.
3
View GPA Chart
A visual chart shows your GPA trend across all semesters that have results published, so you can track your academic progress over time.
Results shown here are fetched live from the LU result server. If a result is not yet published by the university, it will not appear here.

Cover Page Generator

Public
1
Select Cover Type
Choose from available cover page types — Assignment or Lab Report. The template adjusts based on your selection.
2
Fill in Your Details
Enter your name, Student ID, course name, course code, teacher name, and any other required fields. The live preview on the right updates instantly.
3
Download as PDF
Once happy with the preview, click Download PDF. A properly formatted cover page PDF is generated and saved to your device automatically.
The Cover Page Generator does not require login — anyone can use it directly without an account.

Gallery

Public
1
Browse Events
The Gallery shows all class events as album cards — trips, celebrations, and special moments. Click an event card to open its photo album.
2
View Photos
Inside an album, click any photo thumbnail to open it in full screen. Use the arrow buttons or keyboard arrow keys to navigate between photos.
All photos are stored in Google Drive and are loaded on demand, so the page stays fast even with hundreds of photos.

Notifications

Login Required
  • Bell Icon — The bell icon in the top-right navbar opens a dropdown showing your latest notifications. It appears after you log in.
  • Unread Badge — A purple badge number on the bell icon shows how many notifications you have not read yet. It disappears once you open the dropdown.
  • What's New Glow — The What's New link in the navbar glows purple when there are unread portal updates. The glow stops after you visit the What's New page.
  • Routine & Exam Alerts — Enrolled students receive automatic notifications when the class routine or exam schedule is updated.
  • Birthday Notifications — A special birthday card appears when it is a classmate's birthday today, shown automatically on the portal.

What's New

Login Required
  • The What's New page shows a full timeline of all portal updates, organized by version number and release date.
  • Each update is tagged as Feature (new functionality), Improve (enhancement), or Fix (bug fix).
  • Updates you have not seen before are marked with a NEW badge so you can spot what changed since your last visit.
  • Older updates are loaded in pages. Click Load More at the bottom to see the full history.

Portal Status

All Pages

Once logged in, a thin status strip appears just below the top navbar on every page. It tells you the current operational state of the portal at a glance.

Green dot — Portal is fully operational. All features are working normally.
Yellow dot — Portal is up but some features may be limited or slow. Check back later.
Red dot — Portal is under maintenance or temporarily unavailable. Access is suspended.
When the portal is in maintenance mode, a full-screen lock page appears and access is suspended for all users except authorized administrators.

Your Profile

Login Required
  • Click your profile avatar or name in the navbar (visible after login) to open your student profile page.
  • Your profile shows your name, Student ID, photo, department, batch, and other academic details pulled from the university database.
  • To log out, click the logout button on your profile page. Your session data will be cleared from the browser.

Theme & Display

All Pages
1
Toggle Dark / Light Mode
Click the half-circle icon () in the top-right of the navbar to switch between dark mode and light mode.
2
Preference is Saved
Your chosen theme is remembered in the browser. The portal will open in your preferred mode automatically on every visit.

Mobile Navigation — On small screens, the navbar collapses into a hamburger menu (). Tap it to open the full navigation menu as a slide-in overlay.
